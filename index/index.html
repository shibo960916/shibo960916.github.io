I am a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Communication Engineering, Jilin University. My research focuses on evaluation and alignment methods for multimodal models, with an emphasis on human cognitive consistency across visual perception, physical evidence reasoning, and social cognitive reasoning.

I am currently a research intern at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, working on multimodal foundation model evaluation and affective computing. My recent work studies multimodal reasoning, AIGC quality evaluation, human-AI perceptual consistency, egocentric video understanding, multimodal emotion reasoning, and social-order calibration in large models.

  • Affective Computing and Multimodal Emotion Reasoning
