Publications & Research Outputs

My research focuses on multimodal foundation model evaluation, AIGC quality assessment, human-AI cognitive consistency, affective computing, egocentric video understanding, and social cognitive reasoning. Representative works are organized below by research direction.

Note: highlighted papers are accepted papers or officially selected oral presentations.

Direction 1: Multimodal Reasoning & Foundation Model Evaluation
A Brain-Inspired Saliency Prediction Framework for Human-AI Cognitive Consistency in AIGC Content via Multi-Region Liquid Neurons AAAI 2026 Oral 4%
Shibo Wang, Yan Zhao*, Shigang Wang, Jian Wei, Shuo Li
AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence, 2026
A biologically inspired saliency framework that models six human visual brain regions to align AIGC attention with human gaze behavior.
Task AIGC attention consistency
Core idea Multi-region liquid neurons + dual-teacher distillation
Metric HAMCI, NSS, SIM, CC
BAMNet: A Brain Area Mapping-Based Multimodal Saliency Prediction Method ICME 2025 Oral 4%
Shibo Wang*
IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo, 2025
A multimodal saliency prediction network integrating RGB, depth, and eye-tracking data through brain-area-inspired feature fusion.
Input RGB, depth, eye-tracking signals
Output Human attention and saliency prediction
Contribution Brain-area mapping for interpretable multimodal fusion
LifeEval: A Multimodal Benchmark for Assistive AI in Egocentric Daily Life Tasks
