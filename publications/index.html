Publications & Research Outputs

Highlighted papers are accepted or officially selected for presentation.

Direction 1: Multimodal Reasoning & Foundation Model Evaluation

[1] A Brain-Inspired Saliency Prediction Framework for Human-AI Cognitive Consistency in AIGC Content via Multi-Region Liquid Neurons
Shibo Wang, Yan Zhao*, Shigang Wang, Jian Wei, Shuo Li.
AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI), 2026. (Oral, 4%)
Biologically inspired saliency framework modeling six human visual brain regions to align AIGC attention with human gaze behavior. Introduces dual-teacher distillation and HAMCI for quantitative human-AI cognitive consistency evaluation.

[2] BAMNet: A Brain Area Mapping-Based Multimodal Saliency Prediction Method
Shibo Wang.
IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo (ICME), 2025. (Oral, 4%)
Multimodal saliency prediction network integrating RGB, depth, and eye-tracking data via brain-area-inspired feature fusion.

[3] LifeEval: A Multimodal Benchmark for Assistive AI in Egocentric Daily Life Tasks
Hengjian Gao, Kaiwei Zhang, Shibo Wang, Mingjie Chen, Qihang Cao, Xianfeng Wang.
IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2026.
A multimodal benchmark for evaluating assistive AI systems in egocentric daily-life scenarios.

[4] One Battle After Another: Probing LLMs' Limits on Multi-Turn Instruction Following with an Evolving Benchmark Framework
Qi Jia, Ye Shen, Xiujie Song, Kaiwei Zhang, Shibo Wang, Dun Pei, Xiangyang Zhu, Guangtao Zhai.
The 64th Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL main), 2026.
An evolving benchmark framework for probing LLMs in long-horizon multi-turn instruction following.

[5] Beyond Visual Fidelity: Auditing Emotional Reliability in AI-Generated Facial Expressions
Shibo Wang, et al.
Submitted to ACM Multimedia (ACM MM), 2026.
Proposes Real Emotion Probe (REP) to diagnose affective hallucination, manifold drift, anatomical mislocalization, and affective fragility in AI-generated facial expressions.

[6] CECID: Diagnosing and Rectifying Evidence Reasoning Failures in Egocentric Video-LLMs via Causal Evidence Consistency
Shibo Wang, et al.
Submitted to ACM Multimedia (ACM MM), 2026.
Proposes CECID, a framework for diagnosing and rectifying evidence reasoning failures in egocentric Video-LLMs via controlled spatial interventions, E2IC, and SV-EVM.

[7] MERC: Diagnosing Trust Allocation and Reasoning Stability in Multimodal Affective Models under Conflicting Evidence
Shibo Wang, et al.
Submitted to IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing (TAFFC), 2026.
Proposes MERC for auditing trust allocation and reasoning stability in multimodal affective models under controlled cross-modal conflicts.

[8] A Trajectory-Based Framework for Diagnosing and Calibrating Social Order in Large Language Models
Shibo Wang, Yan Zhao, Guangtao Zhai, Peitong Han, et al.
Submitted to Expert Systems with Applications (ESWA).
Introduces a trajectory-based framework and DSOC to quantify and calibrate implicit social-order preferences in large language models.

[9] Learning Human-AI Attention Consistency via Brain-Inspired Saliency Modeling for AI-Generated Images
Shibo Wang, Yan Zhao, Shigang Wang, Jian Wei.
Submitted to Neurocomputing (NC).
Proposes NeuroSalEval, a brain-inspired saliency framework trained via dual-teacher distillation to align model attention with human gaze under domain shift.

Direction 2: Multimodal Perception & Human-Centered AI

[10] CVMNet: A Consistency-Driven Multimodal Framework for Video Popularity Prediction via Multi-Person Perceptual Alignment
Shibo Wang, Yan Zhao, Shigang Wang, Peitong Han, Jian Wei.
Submitted to IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (TMM).
Models multi-person perceptual consensus via spatial-temporal eye-tracking consistency for multimodal video popularity prediction.

[11] Eye-Tracking-Based Perceptual Performance Evaluation for Multi-Screen Spliced Aircraft
P. Han, Y. Zhao, Shibo Wang, et al.
Display, 2026.
An eye-tracking-driven framework for evaluating perceptual performance in multi-screen aircraft display environments.

[13] GNSS-R-Based Significant Wave Height Inversion Using Delay-Doppler Maps

Optics and Precision Engineering, 2022.
Significant wave height inversion using Delay-Doppler maps from GNSS-R observations.

IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo (ICME), 2024.
Fractional-order spectral representations for robust SAR image registration under geometric distortions.

[15] Monitoring Vessel Deadweight Tonnage Using High-Resolution Satellite Images
M. Hou, Y. Li, M. Xie, Shibo Wang, and T. Wang.
Ocean & Coastal Management, 2023.
Satellite image based method for estimating vessel deadweight tonnage in maritime surveillance.

[16] A Quantum-Entanglement-Swapping-Based Key Distribution Protocol for Unmanned Surface Vehicles

Chinese EI Journal.
Secure key distribution protocol for unmanned surface vehicle communication based on quantum entanglement swapping.
