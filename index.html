About Me

Hi, I'm Gongye Liu, a first-year Ph.D. student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Wenhan Luo (main) and Prof. Hongbo Fu.

Prior to that, I obtained my M.Sc degree(2022-2025) in Artificial Intelligence from Tsinghua University, supervised by Professor Yujiu Yang. I received my B.Eng degree(2018-2022) in Beihang University.

I am working on Generative AI, with a focus on diffusion/flow-matching models for images and videos. My interests include foundation models, post-training techniques, and controllable generation.
